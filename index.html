Work

  • A Robot Actor

    Developed for Richard Maxwell's Paradiso, the robot delivers lines, visually tracks fellow actors, and generates a unique script during each performance. Built with Raspberry Pi, Python, Tensorflow, and Arduino. The play was a NYT Critic's Pick.

  • Shared Space

    A minimal platform for collaborative visual research. No longer in use or development.

  • New York City Players

    Typed-while-you-watch homepage for Richard Maxwell's experimental theater company. Designed in collaboration with Scott Ponik and the NYC Players.

  • FuiszVideo

    FuiszVideo: A publishing platform for video ads that uses machine vision to make any object interactive. Frontend development for a huge variety of ad templates, API integrations, and custom interactions.

  • Alex Amini

    A web portfolio for painter Alex Amini. Designed by Rebecca Friedman.

  • Kiria Koula

    A playful online face for Kiria Koula gallery. A flat file CMS and adaptive image serving combine the functions of website files and gallery archives. Designed by Scott Ponik and Julie Peeters.

  • Artspeak

    Brand new website for a beloved Vancouver arts institution. Designed by Scott Ponik and Julie Peeters.

  • American Medium Network

    A Hulu-style online screening room for video art at American Medium gallery.
